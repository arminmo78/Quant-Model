/ Model Architecture

01
Live Market Data
Real-time quotes fetched on every refresh via Yahoo Finance for 18 instruments across equities, ETFs, crypto, and commodities including prices, 1-day change, and moving average positioning.
02
Claude AI Computation
Live market data is passed to Claude AI which simultaneously analyses macroeconomic and technical dimensions, scores each barometer 0–100, and generates a plain-English regime narrative.
03
Dual-Barometer Signal
Both barometers must score ≥60 for BULLISH, ≤40 for BEARISH. Any disagreement defaults to NEUTRAL. The model protects capital first — uncertainty means reduce, not guess.
