/ Model Architecture

01
Macroeconomic Barometer
Monitors yield curve dynamics, credit spread regimes, leading indicators, money supply, and global risk appetite across 18 macro variables. Generates a score from 0–100.
02
Technical Barometer
Evaluates price structure, trend persistence, market breadth, volatility regime, and cross-asset momentum signals. Independent of the macro barometer — both must align before a signal is issued.
03
Signal Generation & Risk Regime
When both barometers agree, a directional signal is generated. Disagreement defaults to NEUTRAL (reduce exposure). The model transitions to cash when macro conditions deteriorate before the technical structure breaks.
